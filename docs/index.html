Welcome

This is the official site for The Threads of Becoming. It surfaces the framework and invites contributions, practices, and community conversation.

The Threads of Becoming is a framework and a structure for identifying and navigation the universal tensions that all humans must navigate as individuals and in communities.

It is intended both for individual study and community growth and conflict enrichment.
