# **Section 2: Cohort Management**

## **Cohort Naming Convention**

Use format: \[Tier\]\[Year\]-\[Sequence\]

Examples: T1-2025-03 (Tier 1, 2025, third cohort), T2-2025-Q2 (Tier 2, 2025, Q2), T3-2025-A (Tier 3, 2025, first cohort)

## **Pre-Cohort Timeline (Tier 2 Example)**

| Timing | Task |
| :---- | :---- |
| 8 weeks prior | Open registration; send announcement to Tier 1 completers |
| 6 weeks prior | Confirm facilitator and supervision team |
| 4 weeks prior | Close early bird registration; first reminder to waitlist |
| 2 weeks prior | Close registration; finalize roster; create cohort platform space |
| 1 week prior | Send welcome packet; materials access; tech check invitations |
| 3 days prior | Final reminder with joining instructions |

## **Supervision Group Assignment (Tier 2\)**

After Week 1, assign candidates to supervision groups of 3-4:

* Balance by professional context when possible  
* Consider time zone compatibility for virtual supervision  
* Assign supervisor from CTF pool  
* Communicate assignments within 48 hours of first session

# **Section 3: Registration & Enrollment**

## **Registration Requirements by Tier**

### **Tier 1: Foundations**

* No prerequisites  
* Registration form captures: name, email, professional role, how they heard about program  
* Payment in full at registration

### **Tier 2: Practitioner**

* Prerequisites: Tier 1 completion \+ professional helping role  
* Additional form fields: Tier 1 completion date, professional credentials, brief statement of interest  
* Deposit at registration; balance due before start

### **Tier 3: Facilitator**

* Prerequisites: CTP certification \+ 1 year practice \+ application  
* Application includes: CV, statement of purpose, supervisor recommendation  
* Application review before enrollment confirmed

## **Waitlist Management**

When cohort reaches capacity:

1. Add to waitlist in registration order  
2. Send confirmation with waitlist position  
3. Notify within 24 hours if spot opens  
4. Hold spot for 48 hours after notification  
5. Roll unplaced registrants to next cohort (with their approval)

## **Cancellation Policy**

| Timing | Refund | Credit Option |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 14+ days before | Full refund minus processing | Full credit |
| 7-13 days before | 50% refund | Full credit |
| \< 7 days before | No refund | Credit for future cohort |
| After start | No refund | Case by case |

## **Virtual Session Setup**

### **Standard Zoom Settings**

* Enable waiting room  
* Enable breakout rooms (pre-assign or allow choice)  
* Enable recording (with notice to participants)  
* Enable closed captioning  
* Co-host: Assign to facilitator and technical support

### **Technical Support Role**

For cohorts of 8+, assign a technical support person to:

* Manage waiting room and admit participants  
* Monitor chat for questions  
* Manage breakout room assignments  
* Troubleshoot participant technical issues  
* Start/stop recording

# **Section 6: Certification Processing**

## **Certification Checklist \- CTP (Tier 2\)**

☐ Completed all four training modules

☐ Attended all three supervision sessions (6 hours)

☐ Submitted practice recording(s)

☐ Submitted written case study

☐ Passed competency assessment (all 5 competencies at 'Meets Standard')

☐ Signed Practitioner Ethics Agreement

☐ Payment completed in full

## **Certification Checklist \- CTF (Tier 3\)**

☐ Completed all six training modules

☐ Completed 20-hour practicum with documentation

☐ Submitted practicum portfolio

☐ Passed live facilitation assessment

☐ Demonstrated teaching competency (observed session)

☐ Signed Facilitator Ethics Agreement

☐ Payment completed in full

## **Certification Number Format**

Format: \[Level\]-\[Year\]-\[Sequential\]

Examples: CTP-2025-0042, CTF-2025-0008

## **Certificate Generation**

6. Verify all requirements complete  
7. Generate certificate with name, designation, number, date  
8. Have authorized signer sign (or digital signature)  
9. Send certificate PDF by email  
10. Update certification database  
11. Add to community platform member list

# **Section 7: Record Keeping**

## **Required Records**

### **By Individual**

* Registration information  
* Payment records  
* Attendance records  
* Supervision logs (Tier 2+)  
* Assessment records and feedback forms  
* Certification status and number  
* Continuing education tracking

### **By Cohort**

* Roster with contact information  
* Facilitator and supervisor assignments  
* Session recordings (if recorded)  
* Attendance summary  
* Completion rates

## **Retention Schedule**

| Record Type | Retention Period |
| :---- | :---- |
| Certification records | Permanent |
| Payment/financial records | 7 years |
| Training attendance | 5 years |
| Session recordings | 90 days (delete after cohort) |
| Practice recordings (candidate) | 90 days post-certification |

## **Privacy & Data Protection**

* Store personal data securely with access controls  
* Never share contact information without consent  
* Honor deletion requests per applicable privacy laws  
* Anonymize data used for program evaluation

# **Section 9: Quality Assurance**

* Registration-to-completion rate  
* Certification pass rate  
* Participant satisfaction scores  
* Community engagement post-certification  
* Recertification rates
